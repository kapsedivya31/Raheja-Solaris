Welcome to Raheja Solaris

About Raheja Universal

Founded in 1980, Raheja Universal is a leading real estate firm in the Mumbai Metropolitan Region (MMR). With its portfolio boasting more than 50 properties, Raheja Universal has won many awards in recent years.
